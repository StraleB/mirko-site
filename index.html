About Me

My name is Mirko, I am 23 years old and I live in Banja Luka, Bosnia and Herzegovina. I am a self-taught photographer and have been awarded on prestigious platforms such as ViewBug, You Pic and the like. My works have been shared by Canon Austria, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Croatia and others.

I am the ambassador of the Sunwayfoto and Daniel Philip brands, and I work with FeiyuTech, Kingston Technology and Skylum Global.
